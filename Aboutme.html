Introduction

Read bellow to learn more about me and my life before starting study at ACU

I am a 20-year-old second-year student studying at ACU. I am undertaking a double degree in a Bachelor of Teaching (Secondary) and a Bachelor of Arts (Technology) to one day become a secondary TAS teacher, specialising in Food and Textiles Technology. I grew up with many supporting individuals in my life, both my family and friends have encouraged me to undertake things that will strengthen me as a person and allow me to grow within myself. I am an avid reader, baker, and animal lover with aspirations to travel the world. My love of travel and culture has been inspired by my childhood in which I got the opportunity to live overseas in both Singapore and China. This has encouraged me to pursue a career pathway that allows me options to live and work in other places around the world, increasing my impact on others.

Makingn an Impact

Making an impact in life has always been something I have strived for, my Christian faith has allowed me to see the benefits of having a strong hope in something other than myself, strengthening my assurance that God knows my pathway in life and will support me every step of the way. I have always wanted to become a teacher and have been heavily influenced by my secondary school teachers as well as others who are teachers themselves. I saw the way I was impacted by my teachers in high school specifically my HSC Food Technology teacher and wanted to create the same impact on others. I want to teach in a way that makes students feel comfortable to learn, experiment within the classroom, encouraging them to become the best version of themselves with the confidence to share ideas and create opinions.
